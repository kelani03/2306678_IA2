About Our Business

At THREADS, we are passionate about bringing out the creativity in you through unique and high-quality crochet and needle kits. Whether you are a beginner or an expert crafter, our curated kits are designed to inspire and help you create beautiful, handmade items with ease and joy. We believe in the power of art and craft to transform lives, and we strive to make it accessible for everyone.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spark creativity and joy through carefully curated crafting kits that bring the art of handmade creation to life. We believe in making crafting an enriching, accessible experience for everyone—whether you're a beginner or a seasoned artisan. Our goal is to provide high-quality materials and easy-to-follow instructions, empowering individuals to discover their creative potential and find relaxation, fulfillment, and a sense of accomplishment in every project. We’re dedicated to fostering a community that embraces the joy of making, one stitch, punch, and weave at a time.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading brand in the crafting world, known for innovation, inclusivity, and fostering a love of handmade artistry. We aspire to create an environment where creativity thrives, connecting people through the shared joy of crafting, while championing sustainability and quality. We envision a future where our kits not only inspire personal growth but also encourage a deeper connection with traditional crafts in a modern world. Ultimately, we aim to transform lives by helping people unplug, unwind, and craft memories that last a lifetime.
